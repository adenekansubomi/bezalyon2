Meet Our Founder

Joshua Oluwatobi Adekanmi
(Broker/Founder)

With over half a decade experience working as a team leader in a top construction company in Kwara state coupled with a solid background experience in the real estate market in Lagos, Joshua started Bezalyon to bring a more personal, value-driven approach to real estate, with the goal to improve professionalism in the sector in Ibadan.
Over the course of his career, Joshua has closed many sales and has represented some big organizations in Nigeria like the Chattered Institute of Personnel Management (CIPM) and others. Working with Joshua will not only produce great results for your deals but also guarantee you peace of mind throughout the transaction process. His genuine care for his clients and attention to details is what makes him an excellent fiduciary. The growing team includes agents and IT team who are committed to upholding the same standards of integrity and service.
